How should onboarding checklists be scoped?

Currently checklists are per-department. You want per-job-title. What's the right model?

A

Job Title Only

Each job title gets its own checklist. Replace department scoping entirely. Simple and direct.

B

Job Title + Global

Job-title-specific checklists plus a global checklist that applies to everyone (e.g. "Sign NDA", "Set up email"). Best of both worlds.

C

Job Title + Department + Global

Most flexible: global steps + department steps + job-title steps all combined. More powerful but more complex to manage.
